Congolese Community Gathering in Malaysia

Reconnect – Register – Be Heard
The Consulate of the Democratic Republic of the Congo in Kuala Lumpur invites all Congolese citizens living in Malaysia to a special community event with a delegation from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs visiting from Kinshasa.

  • Purpose: Reconnect, register with the Consulate, and share your concerns or ideas.
  • Who: All Congolese nationals are welcome.
  • Date: 17th of July 2025
  • Time: 17 : 00 MYT
  • Venue: The Royal Selangor Golf Club, Jalan Kelab Golf Off Jalan Tun Razak 55000 Kuala Lumpur

Meet the Consul, Dato Liew, and our special guests. Let’s strengthen our community and ensure everyone receives the support they deserve.
